What is NPS?
NPS measures customer loyalty by asking how likely someone is to recommend us on a 0–10 scale.
  • Promoters (9–10) = Strong advocates
  • Passives (7–8) = Neutral
  • Detractors (0–6) = Dissatisfied
Calculation: NPS = % Promoters – % Detractors
