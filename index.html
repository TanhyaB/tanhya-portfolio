Qualtrics Customer Feedback Dashboard

The team was tracking customer feedback manually using spreadsheets — it was slow and easy to get wrong. I built a real-time dashboard in Qualtrics from scratch, with automated NPS tracking and sentiment analysis. This gave leadership live data they'd never had before and saved hours of manual work every week.

UX Design

Student Registration Redesign

Prospective students were struggling to find their way into the registration process. I reviewed four key entry pages on the website, scoring each one on clarity, next steps, and how easy it was to register. From this I created low-fidelity wireframes with specific copy and content changes. The next phase involves hi-fi designs to identify missing components in our Drupal CMS, followed by user testing to validate whether the changes get more students into the registration funnel faster.

Navigation & Analytics

Quick Links Navigation

Users were getting frustrated trying to find key pages on the website. I used Google Analytics data to identify exactly where people were getting stuck and what they were searching for. From this I built an implementation plan for a quick links navigation to surface the most important pages and reduce the time it takes users to find what they need.

About Me

I'm Tanhya, a UX Designer based in London. I love finding the messy, complicated bits of a product and making them feel simple and easy to use. Over the last year I've worked across UX design, data dashboards, and platform building — often all at once!

I care a lot about making decisions based on real user evidence, not just gut feeling. When I'm not designing, I'm usually mentoring others or figuring out how to make a process work better.
